3 Conclusion

We can conclude the following Information from the above tables and figures:

  • In Table 2.1, we can clearly notice that males having a private job were the only people affected with stroke out of other categories namely male children and males who never worked.Moreover, out of the affected males mostly all of them were married (Approx. 5.5 % or 57 males).The same can also be clearly seen in Figure 2.1.

  • In Table 2.2, we can clearly notice that males having a private job were the only people affected with hypertension out of other categories namely male children and males who never worked.Moreover, out of the affected males mostly all of them were married (Approx. 11% or 111 males).The same can also be clearly seen in Figure 2.2.

Hence, We can say that both Private job and marriage were the key factors for a male to have stroke or hypertension.
